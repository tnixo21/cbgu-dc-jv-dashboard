CBGU D&C JV - Mechanical & Electrical
Warehouse & Inventory Dashboard  (Cross River Rail)
Snapshot as at 30 September 2024.

HOW TO OPEN
1. Keep index.html and snapshot.js in the SAME folder.
2. Double-click index.html — it opens in your web browser.
3. Keep an internet connection — charts load from a public charting library (Chart.js).
   All data lives inside snapshot.js; nothing is sent anywhere.

TABS
  Overview               Headline KPIs, monthly throughput, accuracy, category & ABC profile
  Purchase Orders        Inbound receipts: POs, lines, units, on-time %, dock-to-stock
  Orders Out             Outbound releases to sites: orders, lines, fill rate, cycle time
  Stock & Articles       Stock by site/category, ABC pareto, status mix, top movers
  Inventory Adjustments  Cycle counts right vs wrong: accuracy, variances, reasons
  Warehouse Analytics    Cumulative flow, ageing, picking workload, ABC, seasonality
  All Articles           Searchable / filterable article master (3,000 SKUs)

DATA
  Article master (3,000 SKUs, categories, suppliers, project-site groups) is the live
  catalogue from Ongoing WMS goods owner 79 (CBGU D&C JV - M&E).
  Inbound / outbound / stock-movement / cycle-count activity is reconstructed across the
  project lifecycle (Aug 2022 - Sep 2024) for analysis and demonstration.
  Metrics are units, movement, counts and percentages only — no pricing or financials.

  Built by Blue Water Shipping.
